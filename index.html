What's the backup for?

This page stores your plants only in this phone's browser — there's no server behind it. That's simple and private, but it means two things:

1. If you clear your browser data, uninstall the browser, or switch to a different phone or browser, your plant list won't be there anymore.

2. It won't automatically show up on a second device, since each browser keeps its own separate copy.

Export backup saves your whole plant list as a small JSON file, so you always have a copy outside the browser. Import backup loads a previously exported file back in — useful after clearing your browser, getting a new phone, or setting the page up on another device.
